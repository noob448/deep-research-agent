# CS:GO Prime Status, Trust Factor & Skin Economy

## Prime Status
- Originally: phone-linked account verification (single account)
- After Dec 2018 F2P: paid players got Prime + loyalty badge automatically
- Later changes (2021): Prime matchmaking access restricted to paid/$15 upgrade only
- Source: src_a0cbbf, src_7050f0

## Trust Factor
- Introduced 2017 by Valve
- Behind-the-scenes reputation score affecting matchmaking quality
- Evaluates: honesty, activity, Steam reputation
- Prime Status boosts Trust Factor
- Source: src_721ea3

## Skin Economy
- 2018: CSGO skins market estimated at ~$1.3 billion (src_f3c0a6)
- CS:GO lifetime revenue: $8-10 billion total (src_7050f0)
- Annual: $1.5-2 billion
- 40+ million copies sold, $600M in game sales
- July 2025: CS2 skin market valued at $5.1 billion (CSMarketCap, src_620e2e)
- Rare skins sold for $100K-$160K+
- Revenue from: game sales, in-game transactions, esports, merchandise

## Operation Riptide (Sept 2021)
- 11th CS:GO Operation
- Lasted until Feb 20, 2022 (16 weeks)
- New features: Riot Shield (CT), Short Match (~25 min), FFA Deathmatch
- Premium Pass: weekly missions, exclusive skins
- Source: src_0c5873, src_cb78f0, src_a3623d
