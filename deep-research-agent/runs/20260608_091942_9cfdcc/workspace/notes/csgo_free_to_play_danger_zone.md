# CS:GO Free-to-Play & Danger Zone (2018)

## Key facts from src_a0cbbf:
- Update 1.36.6.2, December 12, 2018
- CS:GO became free-to-play
- Previous paid players received "Prime" status + loyalty badge
- Previously, Prime required phone-linking to account (verified single account)
- Danger Zone: 18-player battle royale mode on "Blacksite" map
- New skin case also released alongside the update
- Concern: hackers who had previously purchased CS:GO now had access
