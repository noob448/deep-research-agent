# CS:GO Weapons, Maps, Sound Updates (2013-2018)

## R8 Revolver Controversy (December 2015)
- Introduced in 2015 Winter Update (December 2015)
- Extremely overpowered at launch - could one-shot at close range with right-click fan-fire
- Rapidly nerfed within days due to community outrage
- Source: src_7eb595, src_94393f

## AWP Nerf (March 31, 2015)
- Reduced scoped movement speed for AWP and Auto-snipers
- Part of Operation Vanguard period update
- Community reaction: highly controversial, "wormlike scoped movement speed"
- Source: src_e42a84, src_51fccf, src_785812, src_b78a31

## Dust II Remake (2017)
- Feb 3, 2017: Removed from Active Duty map pool, replaced by Inferno
- October 2017: Remade version returned
- Changes: graphical update, new doors, widened Window, simplified Scaffold, A-site Hotel Aurore changes, car repositioned
- Source: src_04e955, src_718eed, src_998c37, src_c6d2b0

## Nuke Remake (2016)
- Released alongside Operation Wildfire (February 2016)
- Complete visual overhaul

## Inferno Remake (2016)
- October 2016 beta, released after community testing
- Source: src_1fcae9, src_b4551c, src_c6d2b0

## HRTF Sound Update (December 2016)
- December 7-8, 2016: HRTF audio processing added to CS:GO
- Improved spatial audio positioning with headphones
- Initial bugs: directionality issues when looking up/down, sound distortion on some weapons
- Fix released Dec 8/9, 2016
- Source: src_b9d7d0, src_dcc1fa, src_b46889
