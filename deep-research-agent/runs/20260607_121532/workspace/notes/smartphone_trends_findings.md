# Smartphone Trends Research - Key Findings

## Global Market
- 2022: <1.2B units, 12% drop (Canalys, src_d34535)
- 2023: ~5% drop predicted (Canalys, src_08c591)
- 2024: 4% growth to 1.17B units forecast (Canalys, src_08c591)
- 2027: 1.25B units, CAGR 2.6% (Canalys, src_08c591)
- KB: 2025 ~1.26B, growth 1.9%

## Replacement Cycle
- Global average: ~3.5 years (Straits Times, src_58f04b)
- Chinese consumers: >40 months (TechInsights via min.news, src_3739f0)
- TechInsights: replacement rate hit bottom in 2023, rebound from 2024 (src_5a0d8a)

## Foldable Phones
- 2022: 12.8M units → 2023: 18.5M projected (TrendForce, src_c98b78)
- Samsung: 82% market share (src_c98b78)
- Three companies control 84% of foldable market (src_d24982)
- China >50% of global foldable shipments by 2024 (src_3ac5f7)
- Samsung Q1 2026: 0.8M units (src_d24982)

## 5G
- End 2023: 1.5B connections, fastest-growing mobile tech (GSMA, src_954cb9)
- Early 2024: 1.6B connections (GSMA, src_cee2b4)
- 2029: 51% of connections; 2030: 56% (GSMA, src_cee2b4)
- 2030: 5.5B connections projected (GSMA, src_cee2b4)
- 2025 market: $15.36B, CAGR 7.25% to $23.38B by 2033 (src_3b2394)

## AI in Phones
- KB: Galaxy AI, Apple Intelligence, Google Gemini replacing hardware as competitive focus
- Gemini Nano on Pixel 10 Pro: ~1000 tokens/sec on-device (src_5533b5)
- Apple working on miniaturizing models for iPhone/Siri (src_8cf794)
- Google Gemini Intelligence: proactive AI on Android (src_0b4567)
- On-device vs cloud trade-off: speed/privacy vs scale/depth (src_5533b5)

## Fast Charging
- Realme: 65W (2021) → 150W (2022) → 240W (2023) → 320W SuperSonic (2024) (src_597fc4, src_28142e)
- Realme GT Neo 5: first 240W charging phone (src_252c4a)
- 240W uses 65W PD among other protocols (src_252c4a)

## AI Hardware Challengers
- Rabbit R1 & Humane AI Pin: both widely considered failures/overhyped (src_887b81, src_c70d06)
- "Solved problems nobody had" (src_c70d06)

## Supply Chain & Geopolitics
- US sanctions on Huawei since Jan 2018 (src_fafec9)
- China highest combination of trade dependency, single sourcing, geopolitical risk (src_051f8c)
- Reshoring from China at all-time high of 87% (src_051f8c)
