# Chinese Smartphone Brands Rise - Research Notes

## Market Share (preliminary)
- 2019 ranking: Samsung #1, Apple #2, Huawei #3, Xiaomi #4, OPPO #5, vivo #6 (same as 2018)
- Q3 2020: Xiaomi surpassed Apple for first time at 13.1% (42% YoY growth per IDC)
- Chinese brands had 81% of India smartphone market at peak (Counterpoint)
- Huawei aimed to overtake Samsung by 2020

## OPPO/vivo
- VOOC flash charging launched 2014 (5V/4A)
- Strong offline retail channel in China
- Both had strong domestic growth per QZ article

## Xiaomi
- Low gross margin strategy, ecosystem of cost-effective products
- Internet marketing, flash sales model
- India market: rapid rise

## OnePlus
- "Flagship killer" positioning
- Started with Snapdragon processors, aggressive pricing
- Later moved toward mid-range/premium

## Transsion (新增)
- 传音控股旗下三大品牌: Tecno, Infinix, itel
- 控制非洲智能手机市场约46%-52%
- 2024年单季出货930万部智能手机到非洲
- 定位: 非洲预算和中端市场
