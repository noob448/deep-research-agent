# Smartphone Research Notes (in-progress)

## Chinese Brands Rise

### Huawei
- Huawei Mate/P series became global flagship competitors
- US sanctions severely impacted Huawei's global smartphone business post-2019
- Honor brand sold by Huawei in November 2020 to allow it to survive independently
- Huawei Mate 20 X 5G went on sale in China Aug 2019 (CNBC, src_3b9fac)
- Mate 70 Pro still launching (ixbt review, src_a93735)

### Xiaomi
- Third-largest smartphone seller globally as of 2025, behind Apple and Samsung
- 754.1M global monthly active users (Dec 2025) (Wikipedia, src_13d2a9)
- Record shipments: 23M phones in Q2 at one point; 49.4M units with 69.1% YoY growth during expansion
- Mi 10 series marked push into premium flagship territory
- IoT ecosystem as key differentiator

### Oppo/Vivo
- OPPO known for fast charging (SuperVOOC), strong offline retail
- Vivo partnership with ZEISS for camera systems (src_3fe895)
- Both strong in China, India, Southeast Asia markets
- 5G phones across budget to premium segments

### OnePlus
- Founded Dec 16, 2013 by Pete Lau and Carl Pei
- OnePlus One launched with Cyanogen OS as "flagship killer"
- Continued growth through 2025 with OnePlus 13/13R series
- Known for competitive pricing with premium specs

### Honor
- Sold by Huawei in November 2020 to Shenzhen Zhixin New Information Technology
- Became top 2 smartphone maker in China by 2022, just 1.5 years after independence
- Valuation exceeded $45 billion by April 2022 (36kr, src_a69177)

## Samsung Galaxy S/Note & Note 7
- Galaxy Note 7 recalled September 2016 - battery fires/explosions
- ~2.5 million devices recalled (BBC, src_9a0cfb; Guardian, src_6d70ce)
- Koh Dong-jin expressed regret; halted sales in 10 markets
- One of the biggest smartphone recalls in history
- Samsung continued Galaxy S series from S5 (2014) through S24 (2024)

## Foldable Phones
- Royole FlexPai: first foldable (2018), but limited impact
- Samsung Galaxy Fold (2019): inward folding, ~$2000
- Huawei Mate X (2019): outward folding, 5G, ~$2400
- Motorola Razr (2019): clamshell/flip design revival
- Samsung Galaxy Z Flip series followed
- KB note: 13.5M global foldable shipments in 2022, but Q3 2024 first YoY decline
- Different form factors: inward fold (Samsung), outward fold (Huawei), clamshell flip (Motorola, Samsung Z Flip)

## 5G
- 3GPP Release 15 specified 5G NR standard
- First 5G phones launched 2019
- Huawei Mate 20 X 5G (Kirin 980 + 5G modem) - Aug 2019 China (CNBC, src_3b9fac)
- Chinese makers (Xiaomi, Oppo, etc.) pledged 5G phones with Snapdragon 855 in 2019 (PCMag, src_d639b9)
- 5G now standard across all price tiers by 2024-2025

## AI in Smartphones
- Apple Neural Engine: since A11 Bionic (2017, iPhone 8/X)
- Huawei Kirin NPU: integrated into Kirin chips for AI acceleration
- NPUs now reaching 100+ TOPS in 2026 smartphones (src_438120)
- Apple Intelligence announced WWDC 2024
- Samsung Galaxy AI launched 2024 (Galaxy S24 series)
- Google Gemini AI integration on Pixel and Galaxy devices
- On-device LLMs becoming key differentiator in 2024-2025
- Google mocking Apple's delayed AI features (Business Insider, src_ab537d)

## Camera Competition
- Multi-camera systems became standard (ultra-wide, telephoto, macro)
- Computational photography driving image quality (HDR+, Night Mode)
- Periscope zoom lenses for 5x-10x optical zoom
- Brand collaborations: Leica (Huawei, Xiaomi), Zeiss (Vivo, Sony), Hasselblad (OnePlus, OPPO)
- Dpreview on Leica smartphone lens engineering (src_76a991)
- Megapixel race alongside sensor size improvements

## Market Data 2024-2025
- 2024 global shipments: ~1.240B units (IDC, +6.4% YoY) / 1.222B (Canalys, +7%)
- 2025: ~1.26B units, +1.9% YoY (IDC)
- Apple ~18% market share 2024 (Counterpoint), 27.6% full year (KB data)
- Samsung regained top spot in Q1 2024
- Xiaomi #3 globally
- Market recovery after 2022-23 inventory correction
- 2025 growth slowed to ~2%
- Q2 2024: 285.4M units, +6.5% YoY (IDC)

## Future Trends

- Silicon-carbon anode batteries for higher density
- AI replacing foldables as key innovation driver in 2024 (Hispotion, src_ac2bdf)
