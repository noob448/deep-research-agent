## HLE (Humanity's Last Exam)
- 2,500 questions across 100+ subjects
- Launched Sep 2024 by CAIS and Scale AI
- Paper: arxiv 2501.14249
- Previous models (GPT-4o, Grok 2, Claude 3.5, etc.) scored very low; o3 scored ~20.32% (from KB)
- Designed as "final closed-ended academic benchmark"

- Survey: "Toward large reasoning models" in Cell Patterns (Oct 2025)
