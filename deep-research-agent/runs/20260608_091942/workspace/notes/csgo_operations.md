# CS:GO Operations 2015-2019 - Initial Notes

## Operation Bloodhound (2015)
- Released: May 26, 2015 - Ended: October 1, 2015
- Maps: Log, Rails, Zoo (and others)
- Most-played operation up to that point
- Introduced the XP/level system (replacing military rank system)

## Operation Wildfire (2016)
- Released: February 17, 2016
- Source: dignitas.gg article

## Operation Hydra (2017)
- Released: May 23, 2017 - Ended: November 13, 2017
- 6 community-created maps, 3 game modes, 6 skin collections
- Predecessor to Operation Shattered Web

## Panorama UI
- Introduced 2018, replacing Scaleform
- Beta: June/July 2018, full release: ~August 2018 (HLTV reports moved out of beta with Horizon Case)
- HTML5/CSS/JS based

## Free-to-Play + Danger Zone
- December 6, 2018
- "Welcome to the Danger Zone" update
